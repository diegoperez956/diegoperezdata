Since the dawn of humanity, we’ve been pattern‑recognizing our way to survival: spotting footprints by watering holes, reading cloud shapes for coming storms, and tracking game across the savannah. It’s an instinct we’ve honed for millennia.

Hi there! I’m Diego, a data scientist who loves turning raw numbers into stories you can actually read. I’ve built everything from Power BI dashboards that unlocked new fundraising insights to Python scripts that automate quarterly reports. Along the way I’ve picked up R, JavaScript, and Tableau, but my true comfort zone is whatever chart helps people say, “Oh, that makes sense!”

I grew up in the Rio Grande Valley, Texas, where I was raised by Mexican immigrants that instilled a hard-working attitude early on in my life. Outside of work you’ll find me nose‑deep in experimental music and art or mid‑rep at the gym. Those passions keep me curious, creative, and always on the hunt for patterns both in data and in life.
